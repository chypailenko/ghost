Blockchain data storage, encryption, and file sharing
Encrypted
Ghost Drive uses a combination of hashing algorithms, ECDSA, and FUSE-based file system and data compression to encrypt your data before atomizing it into thousands of fragments stored on different servers.
Personal Vault
Passwords, records, documents, finances — Ghost Drive is a secure, digital vault that keeps your private information private. No one — including us! — can gain access to your data unless you grant it.
Anonymous Profile
What good is having a safe place to store and share files if your user profile is publicly broadcast? Ghost Drive allows users to maintain anonymity by working behind randomly generated public IDs that encrypt each session.

Features

Ghost Time
You know when they say “This message will self destruct”? Well Ghost Drive allows you to add an expiration clock to anything you share so that when the time is up, the lock resets and no one but you can access it again.
Crypto Wallet
Tired of keeping cold storage? So were we, which is why we made the safest digital wallet that’s ever existed to store and trade cryptocurrencies with ease of mind.
Time Capsule
For an added layer of security, the Ghost Drive time capsule locks files with a smart contract, hiding data from everyone, even you, until a designated time in the future.
Blockchain With Shards
Ghost Drive uses blockchain and sharding technology to ensure that once a user uploads data it remains invisible and can only be unlocked with a private key, which cannot be duplicated, forged, or hacked.
